This is the start of a long one — a journey of recording a hundred songs, however
rough each of them turns out.

The number isn't the point. It's a way to keep showing up and, more than that, to
actually finish things instead of leaving them half-played in a notebook. Some will
be good. Most won't, at least not at first. That's allowed.

I'll write up what I'm learning as I go — the takes that surprised me, the ones I
almost gave up on, and the small things that made a song finally click. For now,
it's just getting started. One song at a time.
